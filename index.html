What ProbQ does for you
🎯

Probable Question Prediction

Every question tracks repeat appearances across previous papers. Higher repeats = higher probability score. Top questions are ranked so you study exactly what matters most.

🤖

AI-Generated 6-Mark Answers

No answer? One tap generates a structured, exam-ready answer — bullet points, key terms, formatted exactly for 6-mark questions. AI also checks student answers and awards a ✅ Verified badge.

📤

One-Tap WhatsApp Share

Share any probable question directly to your batch group — includes question, AI answer, probability % and repeat count. Spreads the knowledge instantly across your entire batch.

📝

Confirmed in Exam!

After your exam, tap the "📝 In Exam?" button on questions that actually appeared. Builds historical accuracy and makes future predictions sharper for every ACOE student after you.

📒

Student Notes Library

Upload and access notes, PDFs and reference material shared by seniors and batchmates — filtered by year, branch and subject. No more hunting in 10 different WhatsApp groups.

📅

AI Timetable Reader

Upload your division timetable as a photo. AI reads it and shows today's schedule — including K1, K2, K3 subdivision-wise practicals. No more confusion about which lab you have.

🃏

Flashcard Revision Mode

Swipe through probable questions like flashcards. Filter by year and branch. Reveal AI answers one by one. The most effective last-night revision tool built for ACOE students.

📄

Printable PDF Export

One click generates a clean A4 PDF of top 15 probable questions for any subject — with AI answers and probability %. Print it and walk into the exam hall prepared.

🔒

ACOE Students Only

No outsiders. No faculty spying. No spam. Post anonymously if you prefer. This is your secret weapon — built and maintained by a fellow ACOE student, not a company.
